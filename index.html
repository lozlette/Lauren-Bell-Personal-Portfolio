ABOUT

Associate Cloud Systems Developer | Junior Full-Stack Web Developer

Hello, I'm Lauren, an Associate Cloud Systems Developer and casual Junior Full-Stack Web Developer based in London and Essex.

As an Associate Cloud Systems Developer at work, and Web Developer in my spare time, I love to learn new skills. I push myself in everything I do, whether it is work-related or hobbies such as coding, running, drawing and painting.

Since first starting to train as a Junior Web Developer, and since becoming an Associate Cloud Systems Developer, I have loved learning new technologies, languages and processes, being able to combine my creative skills with the logic needed to build things from scratch.

I have been at Cloudreach working as an Associate Cloud Systems Developer since 2019, after graduating from General Assembly's Web Development Immersive course. I love being part of a company that provides career paths and opportunities to learn and grow as a developer.

I have an MA in Art Museum and Gallery studies which has influenced me recently in terms of the visual design and user experience of exhibitions, which can be applied to website development.
